Brief Description

Sending a message repeatedly, usually to lots of people at a time. Spamming has lots of uses, usually for malicious intent. Spamming is usually no good, but a keen eye can spot spam easily, and avoid it.

Who started this?

Gary Thuerk

Why does this exist, what do people gain from this?

It exists to mass-promote and to scam people. In commercial use, spam could be used to potentially gain customers, but in it's annoying nature, it may also lose some. In scam use, scammers could gain lots of things, personal info, passwords, bank accounts, locations, etc. all with the click of a link. People who recieve spam, aren't likely to gain anything, rather they may lose something important instead.

Examples From History

They very first spam email (sent by Gary Thuerk) was made to promote the DECSYSTEM-20. May 2000 the ILOVEYOU worm was sent to tens of millions of home computers, faster than any other previous email worm. 2000's were the time spam filters became in use. in 2003 it was the fight to control spam and get it away.

How it affects coding/online culture

If a coder were to get spammed, despite their smart minds, If they were to click a link, they might lose a lot/all progress on a project. Spamming has the same effect on online culture as well, in which usually everyone can agree that it's very annoying and no one likes it when they lose progress/give out personal info
